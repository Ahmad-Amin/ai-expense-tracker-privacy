Privacy Policy

Last updated: Nov 2025

AI Expense Tracker (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information: We may store your name and basic profile info locally on your device.

Expense Data: All expense entries, including merchant, date, amount, category, and notes, are stored only on your device.

Receipt Images: Images are saved locally and never uploaded to any server.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data belongs entirely to you. We use your information only to:

AI Expense Tracker does not send your personal data, images, or receipts to any outside server. All processing (including OCR) is handled through your device or trusted third-party APIs like Google ML Kit or OpenAI only when required.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party libraries for OCR, AI parsing, or purchases. These services may collect limited technical information required to perform their tasks.

5. In-App Purchases

We may offer Premium or Lifetime features through Google Play Billing. We do not store any payment information—Google Play handles all transactions securely.

6. Security

All data is stored locally in secure device storage. We take reasonable measures to protect your information and prevent unauthorized access.

7. Your Rights

  • Delete all your data at any time by reinstalling the app
